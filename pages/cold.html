Certificate Of Ledger Deposits

Fuego's DeFi Banking Suite & Asset DAO

The purpose of COLD Banking Suite is to create an in-house deFi money market for XFG deposit yield.

Allowing XFG to function as an interest-bearing asset, which will not only create additional network utility ( holders can earn tokens (currency, NFTs, etc) as interest on XFG deposit yield- exchangeable in a DeFi environment; without ever trading their XFG ) but more importantly, continues preserving the original hard supply and sound money principals of Fuego.

Details on all off-chain tokens to be offered thru COLD will be listed below.
